Our Culinary Journey

Taste of Kerala in Riyadh

"At Calicut Star, we bring the taste of Kerala to Riyadh with carefully prepared dishes inspired by the rich culinary heritage of Malabar."

Every meal is crafted with passion, tradition, and the warmth of home. We use hand-picked, authentic spices imported directly from Malabar shores to recreate the same nostalgic flavors of traditional Kerala houses. From our flaky porottas to aromatic biriyanis and simmering fish mango curries, we welcome your family to an authentic culinary celebration.
